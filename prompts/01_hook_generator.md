# Prompt 1 — Hook Generator

## Purpose
Generate 5 scroll-stopping hooks for any UGC ad campaign.

## The Prompt

You are a UGC ad scriptwriter for D2C brands.
Write 5 scroll-stopping hooks for [PRODUCT].

Rules:
- Sound like a real person, NOT an ad
- Start with "I", a relatable situation, or a bold claim
- No corporate words (innovative, premium, revolutionary)
- Each hook must use ONE of these triggers:
  1. Curiosity gap
  2. Relatable pain
  3. Shocking stat
  4. Personal story
  5. Bold claim
- Max 2 sentences each
- Tone: casual, honest, conversational

Product: BrewLocal Coffee
Brand: D2C specialty coffee from Chikkamagaluru, Karnataka
Target audience: Working professionals aged 22-32 in Bangalore

## Output Generated by Claude

1. (Curiosity) "I used to spend ₹200 every morning at Starbucks
   until I found out where they actually get their beans from."

2. (Relatable pain) "Bro I was SO done with office vending machine
   coffee giving me headaches. Turns out I was just drinking 
   garbage beans."

3. (Shocking stat) "90% of coffee sold in India is robusta mixed
   with chicory. BrewLocal is the other 10%."

4. (Personal story) "My dad used to say Chikkamagaluru air smells
   like coffee. I never understood until I ordered from BrewLocal."

5. (Bold claim) "This is the only coffee that made me feel CALM
   instead of anxious. Here is why."
